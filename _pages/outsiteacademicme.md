I'm proud to be a Vietnamese girl being raised in the historic capital city, in a traditional and well-educated family. I am a big fan of travelling. I have been to more than 25 countries and still counting. Next on my bucket list: Iceland. 

Apart from doing math and teaching, every day, I enjoy cooking and can spend hours in the kitchen. I feel very relaxed when I cook. I am very good at tasting the food one time and duplicating the dish. I love baking on my family birthdays or some special celebrations. Luckily, my family usually finishes the whole cake with no leftovers!  

I have played the piano since I was 7 years old, and I can play quite well. I love classical music and going to music concerts, which was inspired by my father - the one who taught me how to play the piano for the first time. 

Recently, I start cycling and I am so addicted to it, especially when I live in Edinburgh now - a very beautiful city to cycle around everyda. I also go swimming once every week and quite enjoy playing sports during weekend. I usually do badminton, squash, and I recently have tried bouldering.

I feel quite relaxed when I have time to sit down and start writing, whether it's jotting down everyday thoughts, writing academic papers or writing about any mathematical knowledge I am interested in my own words. I have run a small blog for 8 years where I frequently write about everything happening in my life, including my travels, or simply writing diary during big occasions I want to keep memory. I am also collaborating with my friend to build a blog where we would like to share the beauty of mathematics and skills as a researcher.
